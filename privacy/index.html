Privacy Policy

Tempo — Effective March 30, 2026

Tempo is a home screen launcher for Android developed by Sai Venky. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Information we collect

Tempo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. No data ever leaves your device.

Information stored on your device

Tempo stores the following data locally on your device to provide its features. This data is private to the app and is never transmitted:

  • Your pinned and favorited apps (stored as app package identifiers)
  • Apps you have assigned to time slots (morning, day, evening, night)
  • Apps you have associated with Bluetooth audio playback
  • Custom display names you have given to apps
  • Whether you have seen the Bluetooth permission explanation

Permissions

QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — Required for Tempo to function as a launcher. Android restricts app visibility by default; this permission allows Tempo to enumerate all installed apps and display them in the app drawer. The list of installed apps is used only on-device and is never transmitted.

BLUETOOTH_CONNECT — Used to detect whether a Bluetooth audio device (headphones, earbuds, or speaker) is currently connected, so Tempo can surface the apps you use for audio playback in that context. No Bluetooth device names, addresses, or pairing data are stored or transmitted.

Third-party services

Tempo contains no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, advertising networks, or crash reporting services.

Children's privacy

Tempo does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app collects no personal data from any user.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
